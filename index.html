About Extra University

Innovative Education Since 2020

Extra University was established in Almaty, Kazakhstan with a vision to revolutionize higher education by combining academic excellence with practical, industry-relevant skills.

Our modern campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, collaborative learning spaces, and a vibrant international community.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • High school diploma with minimum GPA 3.45/5.0
  • IELTS 5.0+ or equivalent English proficiency
  • Passport copy and 2 passport photos

Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • IELTS 6.0+ or equivalent English proficiency
  • 2 letters of recommendation
